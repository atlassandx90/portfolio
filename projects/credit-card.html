Credit Card Customer Analytics

An end-to-end credit card customer analytics project developed using Python, SQL and Power BI to analyze customer demographics, spending behaviour, transaction patterns and attrition risk. The interactive dashboard provides valuable insights for data-driven business decisions.

Business Problem

Banks collect massive volumes of customer transaction data every day, making it difficult to identify customer behaviour, spending trends and potential churn. This project transforms raw transaction data into interactive dashboards to support strategic business decisions.

Project Workflow

Data Collection

Collected customer profiles, transaction history and credit card usage data from multiple banking datasets.

Data Cleaning

Handled missing values, standardized customer records and prepared datasets using Python.

SQL Analysis

Analyzed customer spending patterns, income groups, transaction behaviour and card performance using SQL.

Power BI Dashboard

Built interactive dashboards with KPIs, customer segmentation, attrition analysis and predictive insights.

Business Insights

Customer Spending

High-income customers generated significantly higher transaction values than other customer groups.

Customer Behaviour

Customers with frequent transactions demonstrated higher retention and long-term profitability.

Card Performance

Premium credit cards generated higher average spending while standard cards maintained higher customer volume.

Business Recommendation

Target high-risk customers with personalized offers and reward programs to reduce attrition and improve customer lifetime value.
